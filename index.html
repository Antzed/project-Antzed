The users for our application will be hikers and anyone who wants to get into hiking. It will allow them to determine which hike would be perfect for them. If you are new to Washington or live here and want to go on a hike, you can just use the website, and it will help you figure out what hike you are looking for. Our users will be able to see information like fun places to hike and uploaded outdoor images of hikes. You will be able to see information about the hike, what route to take, difficulties, elevation, and distance. Also, users can upload data about closed roads or hikes. This website will solve the problems by making it easier for people to go hiking and find hikes. It will also provide them with all the information in one without having to look elsewhere.
